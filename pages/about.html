About Us.

We're on a mission to make lending better for everyone by bringing speed and simplicity to the mortgage journey.

Mast frees up lenders to process more high-quality applications, supports brokers by offering an intuitive and user-friendly platform, and benefits buyers by helping them obtain a mortgage faster.

We're building an ecosystem of world-class mortgage technology.

It's an open secret that the mortgage journey is painful and slow for many people in the UK. Some borrowers have to wait over 60 days for a decision on their mortgage application.

With the right technology, we can improve the experience for everyone involved. For brokers, we can make lenders easier and simpler to do business with. For lenders, we can help smooth operations and get the right information into the hands of those who need it faster. And for the customer, their clunky stressful applications can be made a thing of the past.

The Mast Team

Mast’s HQ is in London, and our team is a combination of experienced finance professionals and world-class software engineers working collaboratively side-by-side.

Our Product and Management teams have experience in Digital Banking, Mortgage Lending, Commercial Banking, and Capital Markets. Our engineers have previously worked for industry-leading fintechs and cloud computing companies. They have a wide range of skills that allow us to build slick, stable and secure technology.

We’re pooling together our experience to bring the best practices from each of these fields to mortgage technology.

As a team, we focus on listening to and working closely with our clients to ensure we completely understand their pain points before we start building features. Our spotlight on customer feedback at every level of the organisations we work with helps ensure we’re as efficient as possible in delivering a great product.
